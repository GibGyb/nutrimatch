How It Works

Three simple steps to age-perfect nutrition.

01

Define Your Profile

Nutritional needs shift dramatically from growth phases in teens to metabolic maintenance in seniors. We start with your age group to set the right baseline.

02

Scan or Pick

Upload a photo of a restaurant menu or your dinner plate. Our AI identifies ingredients and estimates caloric density in real-time.

03

Get Your Match

We cross-reference the food data with age-specific medical guidelines to give you a 'Match Score' and personalized health recommendations.

Why Age Matters?

Different life stages require different macro-nutrient ratios. Seniors need higher protein to combat muscle loss (sarcopenia), while Kids require specific healthy fats for brain development. NutriMatch uses advanced data modeling to ensure your plate matches your biology.
